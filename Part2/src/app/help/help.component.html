Help & FAQs

How to add a new product?

Go to "Manage Products" from the navigation bar, fill in the form (required fields are marked), and click "Save Product".

How to edit a product?

Go to "All Products", click the "Edit" button next to the product. You will be redirected to the form with data pre-filled; modify and save.

How to delete a product?

In "All Products", click the "Delete" button and confirm the action.

Why is data lost after closing the browser?

This is a pure front-end demo; data is only stored in memory. For persistence, a back-end database would be needed in future versions.

How does search work?

Enter a product name keyword (case-insensitive) on the Search page, then click Search or press Enter. All products whose names contain the keyword will be displayed.

How to view popular items?

On the "All Products" page, click the "Show Popular Only" button to filter.
